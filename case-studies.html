Name: Strange Terrariums

Date: 03/30/2026

Services: UX Design | Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) | Form Services*

The wonderful client needed and engaging and welcoming platform where she could get her clients to fill out forms to request orders for her terrariums. Whether for a workshop, or a form event, the customer needs to be able to access and complete the form that meets their needs. In addition, the client needed to be able to let her customers know where they could find her for in-person events. Client came with their brand colors, and SoftSolves provided the UX Design, mobile responsiveness, accessibility, and a wee bit of whimsy!

*Form Services through open-source product Web3Forms
