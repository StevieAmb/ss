SoftSolves is a website design, development, and maintenance business for other businesses who want their customers to be invested in their success.

GOSH, HOW DO YOU DO THAT?

Oh, well we serve you with:

Design

It begins with a wireframe, which leads to an innovative design of your website. The design incorporates copy, images, layout, colors, and innovation that screams to the client the unique personality of your business.

Development

In this step, we take the design and build it. We can do coding from scratch, Shopify, Systeme.io, Squarespace, Wix, Wordpress, GHL, etc. Your website will look and work amazing, regardless of your pick.

Accessibility

People of all abilities should be able to navigate through your website, and get the information they need. From color contrasts, to semantic HTML, to properly named hyperlinks, we make sure your website is accessible to all.

Mobile Responsiveness

About 40% of folks access a website on a mobile device. Your business' website should look good there, too. At SoftSolves, we make sure that's the case, designing for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Maintenance

Need new photos? Need to update your copy? SoftSolves is here for that work, too. For an additional price, we keep all of your content relevant to where your business stands, currently.
